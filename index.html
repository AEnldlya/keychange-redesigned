Our Mission

Bridging the gap in music education

Founded by flute players Jason Wang and Ansh Mishra, Key Change collects instruments, music supplies, and donations for schools lacking resources.

We aim to make musical instruments and resources available to schools lacking funding, and to create inclusive communities where ambitious students can pursue music.

What We Do

How we make music accessible

🎺

Instrument Collection & Redistribution

We collect donated instruments and music supplies and redistribute them to schools and students who lack access.

🏫

Lowering Cost Barriers

High equipment costs are the leading cause of music program budget cuts. We work to remove that barrier for schools and students.

📣

Volunteer Recruitment & Awareness

We recruit volunteers and raise awareness of music access challenges through our newsletter and community partnerships.
