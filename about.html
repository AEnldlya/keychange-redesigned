Who We Are

About Key Change

A student-led initiative on a mission to expand access to music education — one instrument at a time.

Our Mission

To expand the accessibility of music education

Key Change's mission is to expand the accessibility of music education and build a team of people committed to sustaining this effort.

We aim to make musical instruments and resources available to schools lacking funding, and to inspire others to donate unused instruments and educational materials.

We work to challenge the barriers that discourage talented students from pursuing music education.

🎼

Our Story

How it all started

🎶

Jason Wang and Ansh Mishra, both flute players, noticed disparities in instrument access among their peers. They created Key Change to collect donated instruments and supplies and distribute them to under-resourced schools and students.

Why It Matters

The power of music education

Music participation enhances mental health, fosters creativity and self-expression, and develops teamwork, communication, and confidence.
