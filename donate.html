Help more students find their own joy of music. Donate an instrument, supplies, or funds — every contribution matters.

What to Donate

We collect instruments & music supplies

Key Change collects working instruments and music supplies to provide opportunities for students who would not otherwise have access to music education.

🎷

Instruments

Playable instruments of any kind — wind, string, percussion, keyboard. Must be in playable condition or need only minor maintenance.

🎵

Music Supplies

Reeds, tuners, and other music supplies are always welcome — even small items make a difference.

How to Donate

Three ways to get instruments to us

📍

In-Person Drop-Off

Drop your instruments or supplies at one of our partner locations. Contact the location in advance to arrange your drop-off.

Our Savior Lutheran Church

Contact: PastorKyle@oslchanover.org

Norwich Congregational Church

Contact: norwichcongregational@gmail.com

📬

Mail Small Items via USPS

Small items like reeds, tuners, and sheet music can be mailed directly to our P.O. Box using USPS.

Mailing Address (USPS)
Key Change
P.O. Box 997
Hanover, NH 03755

Please complete our donation tracking form before shipping so we can confirm receipt.

📦

Ship Large Instruments via UPS or FedEx

Large instruments that can't be mailed via USPS should be shipped via UPS or FedEx to our street address.

Shipping Address (UPS / FedEx)
Key Change
50 S Main St
Hanover, NH 03755

Requirements for instrument donations:

  • Instruments must be playable or require only minor maintenance
  • Ship in a hard-shell case with adequate protective padding
  • Complete the donation tracking form before shipping
  • Contact us if your instrument needs major repairs — we'll advise on next steps

Have questions about donating?

Not sure if your instrument qualifies? Unsure about shipping? We're happy to help. Reach out and we'll walk you through the process.
